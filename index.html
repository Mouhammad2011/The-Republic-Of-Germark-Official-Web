About Germark

Germark is a ceremonial micronation founded in Haderslev, Denmark, by Mouhammad and Björn in 2025. It represents shared leadership, civic imagination, and peaceful sovereignty. Germark’s territory rotates daily between its co-presidents, and its Constitution affirms symbolic citizenship and cultural diplomacy.

Citizenship
