About Germark

Founded in Haderslev, Denmark, Germark is a ceremonial micronation established by Mouhammad and Björn in 2025. It began as a symbolic colony and is preparing to declare full independence in December 2025. Germark’s territory rotates daily between its two co-presidents, reflecting shared leadership and hybrid sovereignty.

Constitution

The Constitution of Germark outlines its dual presidency, symbolic citizenship, and ceremonial legal system. It affirms our commitment to peace, creativity, and civic imagination.
